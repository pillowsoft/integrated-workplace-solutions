About IWS

IWS (Integrated Workplace Solutions) is an outcome-based network of medical providers that statistically show greater outcomes with medical recovery, disability duration and fewer services per claim. This has resulted in a cost savings of over 50% per claim.

  • We allow for an increased claim savings through improved medical control within our own network to improve return to work outcomes.
  • We allow better control over individual program components and the management of overall claim outcomes.
  • We are able to tailor services to meet employer’s direct needs. It allows a company options that will produce the best treatment outcomes for the injured worker with the lowest medical costs.
  • We provide the best outcomes for the injured workers with a clear plan of what services are necessary with associated costs. We have transparency both financially and clinically.

How do we get over 50% cost savings per claim?

We utilize our proprietary delivery model that allows for a comprehensive, effective and efficient service without sacrificing care or safety.
